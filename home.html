Our History

Squirrels Tree Service Inc is a tree service company located in Zion Rd Zion, IL. Founded in 1999 by a team of professionals passionate about tree care and the environment, the company has experienced tremendous growth and success over the past few decades. In its early years, they focused on offering tree trimming and removal services, which allowed them to establish a solid reputation in the local community. Over time, Squirrels Tree Service Inc expanded their range of services to include land clearing, limb grinding and emergency care. Thanks to its commitment to customer satisfaction and the quality of its work, the company has become one of the most trusted tree service companies in the region. Today, Squirrels Tree Service Inc is known for its ability to tackle projects of any size and scope, and is proud of its contribution to the beauty and health of the natural environment of the Rd Zion, IL community.
